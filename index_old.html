About Me

I'm Jaya Sandeep, an unwavering and dedicated individual with a solid foundation in computer science. I find my passion and thrive in the dynamic and challenging realms of Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence. Currently, I'm pursuing my academic journey at prestigious Indiana University Bloomington, building upon the strong educational foundation I received at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology(IIT), Bhilai.


My quest for knowledge has equipped me with a robust understanding of the core principles that drive the world of computer science, serving as a launch pad for my professional pursuits. My interests span a wide spectrum, encompassing software development, Data Science, and the ever-evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence

In this capacity, my expertise in Python, Pyspark, Azure, SQL, and more played a pivotal role in tackling complex challenges and crafting effective solutions.


The inherent capacity of data to unveil valuable insights and guide decision-making has always been a source of fascination for me. Driven by an unquenchable thirst for knowledge and bolstered by my technical proficiency and industry experience, I am confident in my ability to excel in my role and make meaningful contributions in the field.


As I continue on my journey, I look forward to new opportunities, challenges, and collaborations that allow me to harness my skills and expertise for the betterment of the ever-evolving world of technology and innovation.

Trans Lingua Script

×

Trans Lingua Script

Developed a model which can detect the signboards, extract the text from the signboards and convert it into Hindi language.


Developed an object detection model to detect sign board objects in a given image and a CNN-RNN encoder decoder model to convert the cropped signboard into text and later used a sequence to sequence with attention model for transliterating the above sequence of characters from English to Hindi language.


Achieved an accuracy of around 90% and developed using Python, TensorFlow, Keras and Natural Language Processing.
